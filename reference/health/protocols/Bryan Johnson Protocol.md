# Bryan Johnson Protocol

## What It Is

A public, systems-oriented health and longevity protocol published by Bryan Johnson. It is best treated here as a high-signal external reference, not as a personal health plan.

## Why Keep It

- It is unusually structured and comprehensive.
- It can serve as a source of concrete habits, heuristics, and protocol ideas.
- It is useful as a benchmark or comparison point for sleep, nutrition, exercise, and measurement-heavy health systems.

## Main Areas Covered

- sleep
- exercise
- nutrition
- skin
- oral care
- hair
- clean water
- measurement
- daily routine
- advanced therapies

## Most Useful General Takeaways

- Treat sleep as foundational rather than optional.
- Build health around systems and routines instead of daily willpower.
- Reduce decision fatigue by standardizing recurring behaviors.
- Use measurement where it improves feedback, but do not confuse tracking with actual progress.

## How To Use This In PeterOS

- Use it as a reference source, not as a default instruction set.
- Borrow specific ideas selectively.
- Move any personally adopted habits into your own health knowledge or records later.
- Keep advanced or experimental sections at arm's length unless you have strong reason to dig deeper.

## Caveats

- This is not neutral medical consensus guidance.
- The protocol mixes accessible habits with more extreme or experimental elements.
- The site is dynamic, so details may change over time.
