# Philosophy And Politics

Use this folder for durable worldview pages, public-thinking topics, and synthesized positions that persist beyond one reading session.

Messier active research should begin in `research/`.

## Pages

- [[Beliefs As Interpretive Tools]] - model for treating beliefs as perception-shaping tools and using alternative interpretations to recover agency.
- [[Human Provenance After AI Abundance]] - model for why human-created work may retain value under AI abundance through provenance, status, beauty, trust, and shared experience.
- [[Stated Life Goals And The Funeral Test]] - practice of writing explicit life-goal essays so days can be judged against chosen aims rather than only external expectations.
- [[The Great Filter]] - inference from the apparent absence of visible cosmic colonization to a major barrier somewhere between dead matter and expanding lasting life.
