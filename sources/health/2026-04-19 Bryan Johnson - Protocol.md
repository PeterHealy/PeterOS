# Bryan Johnson Protocol

## What This Source Is

Bryan Johnson's public protocol site, presented as a broad longevity and health system. It combines top-level habit advice, structured protocols, and a large amount of measurement-oriented personal experimentation.

## Key Ideas

- Sleep is treated as the foundational behavior that improves many other health outcomes.
- The site organizes health into protocols rather than isolated tips, with sections for sleep, exercise, nutrition, skin, oral care, hair, water, and measurement.
- The overall style is systems-heavy: reduce decision fatigue, build routines, measure aggressively, and rely on repeatable habits rather than motivation.
- The protocol blends relatively mainstream habits with more advanced or experimental interventions.

## Claims Worth Tracking

- Lowering resting heart rate before bed is framed as one of the highest-leverage practical actions for better sleep.
- High-quality sleep, nutrition discipline, and regular exercise are presented as the core accessible habits.
- Measurement is treated not as optional tracking but as a central design principle for health improvement.

## Caveats

- This is a public protocol assembled around one person's philosophy and experiments, not a neutral medical guideline.
- Parts of the site are practical and accessible, but parts extend into advanced therapies or strong claims that should be evaluated carefully.
- The source is best used as a structured reference and idea library, not as something to adopt wholesale without judgment.
